Cedric's Time Capsule

Cedric is from Sacramento, California. Cedric enjoys many different slices of life. He often likes to cook. Some of the meals he likes to cook include steak, spaghetti, lasagna, and hamburgers. Another thing he likes to do is watch action sports. He watches skateboarding, motocross, and surfing. When he was younger, Cedric used to skateboard , but he was not exceptionally good at it. Cedric enjoys other sports as well. He consistently watches basketball, football, and soccer. Cedric’s favorite soccer team is Club America from Mexico City.Cedric's favorite sports players are Bam Adebayo from the Miami Heat and De'Aaron Fox from the Sacramento Kings.

Cedric watches the WNBA, as supporting women’s sports is important to him. His favorite players in the WNBA are Aja Wilson and Brianna Stewart. Cedric likes watching a variety of anime. Some of his favorites are Yasuke and Akira. Cedric is studying Visual Communications and Design. He is interested in interactive design focusing on the deaf community. Cedric is always busy with school but finds time to spend with his family. He likes to go to family cookouts and spend the holidays with them. One of Cedric’s other hobbies is science; he hopes to make animations that feature black scientists. Cedric used to work as a tutor for different after-school programs.

Cedric used to play video games some of his favorites were Streetfighter alpha, Robotech and Ninja turtles. He likes to draw and paint when he has the time and space. Cedric enjoys music sometimes, some artists he likes are Jadakiss, Styles P, Robert Glasper, Wayne Porter, Donald Bryd and E-40. Cedric likes movies, he has watched Coming to America, Last Black Man in San Francisco and Antman several times. Cedric is trying to learn Spanish for a trip to Mexico.
